# Demo Script: AdaptiveDrive AI

This script outlines the flow for a 3-minute demo video.

## 1. The Problem (0:00 - 0:30)

- **Visual**: Text overlay or slide with stats.
- **Narration**: "Over 600,000 people in the United States drive with adaptive hand controls. Learning to operate a push-pull lever for throttle and brake takes practice, and real-world practice can be stressful. What if you could build that muscle memory safely, with an AI coach watching your every move?"

## 2. Meet AdaptiveDrive AI (0:30 - 0:50)

- **Visual**: Show the landing screen with the 3D road scene, trees, and the "Start Training Session" button.
- **Narration**: "AdaptiveDrive AI is a multimodal training simulator. No special hardware. Just your webcam, your hand, and an AI driving instructor powered by Gemini."
- **Action**: Click "Start Training Session."

## 3. Hand Tracking in Action (0:50 - 1:20)

- **Visual**: The camera feed activates. Show hand in front of webcam. The steering wheel overlay rotates as the hand moves.
- **Action**: Pull hand back. Show throttle bar filling up and the car accelerating on screen. Push forward. Show brake bar responding.
- **Narration**: "MediaPipe tracks your hand in real time. Pull back to accelerate, push forward to brake, move left and right to steer. The 3D environment responds instantly. No controllers, no setup."

## 4. AI Coaching (1:20 - 2:15)

- **Action**: Select "Approaching a Stop Sign" scenario.
- **Visual**: The scene shows trees, buildings, and a stop sign ahead. The distance counter appears.
- **Narration**: "Each scenario comes with a live AI coach. Gemini watches your hand data and coaches you through the maneuver."
- **Visual**: The AI transcript shows coaching: "Start easing off the throttle..." then "Begin braking now..."
- **Action**: Demonstrate a smooth stop. AI provides positive feedback.
- **Action**: Demonstrate a hard brake. AI says it was too sharp, recommends a gradual approach.
- **Narration**: "The AI adapts its coaching to what you actually do. Too fast, too sharp, missed the line. It tells you, and it tells you how to improve."

## 5. Multimodal Power (2:15 - 2:40)

- **Visual**: Show the "Scenario Mode" toggle. Show the loading spinner as Gemini generates a scene background.
- **Narration**: "In Scenario Mode, Gemini generates photorealistic driving environments that match each exercise. Computer vision for your hands, 3D rendering for the road, generative AI for coaching and scenery. All connected in real time."

## 6. Closing (2:40 - 3:00)

- **Visual**: Show all three scenario cards. Show the architecture diagram.
- **Narration**: "AdaptiveDrive AI is built with React, Three.js, MediaPipe, and the Gemini Live API. It runs in the browser, deploys to Cloud Run, and needs nothing but a webcam. Because driving is freedom, and everyone deserves a patient coach."
- **Visual**: Logo and tagline.
